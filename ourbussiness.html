As a global leader in the energy sector, we operate across the entire value chain - from exploration to distribution. With over 50 years of industry experience, we've developed advanced technologies and sustainable practices to responsibly develop energy resources that power economies and communities worldwide.

Our operations span across 15 countries with 32 active production fields and 4 major refineries. We maintain a diverse portfolio of both conventional and unconventional resources, leveraging cutting-edge techniques to maximize efficiency while minimizing environmental impact.
