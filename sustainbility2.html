Governance & Ethics

At Global Oil & Gas, we maintain the highest standards of corporate governance and ethical conduct. Our governance framework ensures accountability, transparency, and responsible decision-making at all levels of the organization.

Our Code of Business Conduct and Ethics applies to all employees, officers, and directors, providing clear guidelines on ethical behavior, conflicts of interest, and compliance with laws and regulations. We conduct regular ethics training and maintain confidential reporting channels for any concerns.
